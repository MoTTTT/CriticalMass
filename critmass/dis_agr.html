The topic of this distribution agreement is

    A range of items produced by the VENDOR bearing the "Critical Mass" brand,
    including the Compact Disk entitled "Critical Mass Volume 4", available immediately, and
    further CD volumes and books as produced by the VENDOR bearing the Critical Mass brand.
    Items to be produced by the VENDOR in the Critical Mass range will include distributions of the Linux operating system, and applications for the Linux operating system.

herinafter called the PRODUCT.

The VENDOR and the DISTRIBUTOR agree as follows:

  1. The VENDOR appoints the DISTRIBUTOR as the sole distributor of the PRODUCT.

  2. The appointment is valid for all territories in which the DISTRIBUTOR operates.

  3. The period of this agreement is one year, starting 15 July 1999.

  4. The DISTRIBUTOR will have the option of extending the sole distribution rights for a second period of one year if the specified sales target of 5000 units for the first period are met.

  5. This agreement can be terminated before the specified expiatory date by either party giving 30 days written notice.

PRODUCT category, price structure, warranty, product obsolescence, and stock rotation

  1. The "Critical Mass Volume 4" PRODUCT is a CD containing a distribution of the Linux operating system.

  2. This CD will be available for R60-00 per unit for quantities less that 100. Orders of 100 and above will be at R55-00 per unit.

  3. Quoted prices include VAT. All prices are quoted in South African currency.

  4. A document describing the PRODUCT, and it's pricing structure will be provided to the DISTRIBUTOR when products are added, or the pricing structure changes.

  5. This will be made available 30 days before becoming effective if changes to the pricing of currently stocked items are effected.

  6. Defective PRODUCT items may be returned for replacement.

  7. If items are added to the PRODUCT range that cause items in stock to become obsolete, these items will be replaced within a period of one month after the new item is available.

  8. If stock of a particular item in the PRODUCT range is held by the DISTRIBUTOR for more than six months, then the DISTRIBUTOR may return such stock. The maximum number of such items returned will amount to 20% of the number  of items purchased from the PRODUCT range in the previous quarter.

PRODUCT orders, terms, credit limits and shipment

  1. Orders are to be placed by faxing an order request to Q Solutions.

  2. Payment terms will be 45 days from statement, with interest at prime rate on overdue accounts.

  3. A credit limit of R20 000-00 will be reviewed within two months of signature of this agreement.

  4. The VENDOR will deliver the PRODUCT to the DISTRIBUTOR's premises at the VENDOR's cost and risk.

  5. The delivery address will be the DISTRIBUTOR's Johannesburg address for large consignments, with the option of delivery to a Cape Town address for order quantities of less than 200 CDs.

  2. The software contents of the PRODUCT are copyrighted by their respective owners, and licensed in terms of Open Source Licenses, including the GNU General Public License. Copies of these licenses are included with the product.

  3. Items to be published by the VENDOR under the Critical Mass brand will be exclusively Open Source Licensed items.

  4. The re-distribution of the contents of the PRODUCT by the VENDOR is explicitly allowed and encouraged in terms of the licenses used.

  5. The VENDOR, the DISTRIBUTOR and its retailers, and the retailer's clients have identical rights concerning re-distribution of the contents in terms of the Open Source license.

  6. This agreement between VENDOR and DISTRIBUTOR does not impact on the terms of the licensing of the contents of the PRODUCT, nor do those licenses impact on the terms of this agreement.
